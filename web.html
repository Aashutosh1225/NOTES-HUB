ABOUT NOTESHUB

NotesHub is a platform that connects:

  • Creators: Share your knowledge by uploading educational notes
  • Readers: Access high-quality notes to enhance your learning

OUR MISSION

To create a collaborative learning environment where knowledge is shared and accessible to all.

HOW IT WORKS

For Creators

  1. Create an account
  2. Upload your notes in PDF format
  3. Set your pricing (optional)
  4. Share your knowledge with the community

For Readers

  1. Create an account
  2. Browse available notes
  3. Purchase or download free notes
  4. Enhance your learning experience

WHY CHOOSE NOTESHUB?

  • High-quality, curated educational content
  • Secure and easy-to-use platform
  • Mobile wallet integration for seamless payments
  • Community-driven knowledge sharing
